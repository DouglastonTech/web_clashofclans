Resources
Dark elixir is used to upgrade and buy heroes. You can also train dark troops with dark elixir. You can store it in your dark elixir storage and collect it in your dark elixir drills
Gold is used to buy and upgrade defenses, rebuild  your clan castle and upgrade it and rearm traps. Gold is stored in your gold storage's and is collected in your mines for your storage's.
Elixir is used to upgrade your troops in the lab. The elixir storage is used to store your elixir.
The elixir collector collects elixir for your storage's.
It is also used to train troops.
Green gems are used to speed up upgrading and building. It is used to speed up training troops to. You can get gems from removing obstacles.

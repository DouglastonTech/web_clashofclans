Buildings
Your Town Hall is very IMPORTANT. It unlocks you new buildings when you upgrade it. When someone is attacking and they destroy your town hall they get one star. Town Hall level 10 is the highest level a Town Hall can get.
The Lab is used to research your troops. It makes them stronger and do more damage. You can upgrade it all the way to level 8.
The Barracks is used to train troops. If you upgrade the Barracks you can train different troops. You can upgrade the Barracks to level 10.
The Spell Factory is used to create spells that may come in handy during battle. Spells may change the tide of the battle.

Clan castle is where your loot bonus and troops that your clan mates donated are stored. upgrade it to increase how much capacity of troops it could hold.
The Army Camp is where your troops wait for you to attack, when you upgrade it, it will gain 5 more capacity.
Your builders live in these. Nothing gets done without builders. You can speed up their work with green gems. You can buy more of them to build or upgrade more things at once. It costs gems.
